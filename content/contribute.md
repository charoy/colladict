Contributions flow through GitHub so that proposals, revisions and disagreements remain visible.

## Propose a new entry

Open a `New entry` issue and describe the term, why it matters for CSCW and a few starting references.

## Raise a correction or debate

Open a `Correction / debate` issue to flag a problem, omission, contestable claim or missing source.

## Submit a direct change

Create a branch, edit or add a file in `content/entries/`, then open a pull request.

Contributions in English or French are welcome.
