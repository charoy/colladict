The dictionary does not aim for strict encyclopedic neutrality. It adopts a critical, situated and revisable perspective.

Each entry can combine:

- an operational definition;
- a genealogy or context of emergence;
- theoretical or political tensions;
- links to related concepts;
- a short bibliography.

Because the site is edited in Git, disagreement can remain productive: proposal, discussion, revision and traceability are part of the editorial method.
